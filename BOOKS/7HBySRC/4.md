## Author's Note
In chapter 2, part II of the book, Stephen insist on start building life keeping the end in mind. 
He asks the readers to answer a profound question at their own death day expectation like how they want their loved ones to remember them?
This thought process is to help the readers to create a solid principles to work with. Based on the the human requirement at death bed build the concept 
of ones life.

With the end requirements at hand, using endowed imagination, freedom, conscience build a map, compass and vision to become an effective person.
Everything in this world is build twice, ones in mind and second physically. To build an effective life use the proactive paradigm build a model with
end in mind then physicall bring it using the first think first approach.

Leadership and management are two important skills to be developed to move from dependent to indepedent level in the maturity. 


## Personal Opinion
- It reminds me of system building based on requirements, based on the given requirements build the system. Similarly the author asks people about their requirement.
- From a engineering background I can understand the need of building an model to validate first the idea and going for the real one.
