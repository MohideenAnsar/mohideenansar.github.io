## Author's Note
Synergy is like finding an out-of-box win/win solution to a proble with participation of two/more interdependent people. It is the outcome of integration of all previous habits, the four endownments, emphathetic listening
and win/win approach. Building an emotional bank deposit is foremeost important in this process, as it will open the communication.

In an organisation or an family, we generally admire the difference in physical nature. But the intellectual, emotional and phsycological differences often seen as a conflict instead of admiring and understanding it.
