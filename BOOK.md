## Description

This blog is about the book "7 habits of Effective People" written by Stephen R Covey.

This blog series consists of explaination of the authors note (description of the habits/principles) and highlight my opinion/observation of the principle with real time implementation for each part of the book.

All credits of the principles goes to the original Author of the book. My personal opinion/observation are exploratory nature and my personal notes.
